Privacy Policy

Shears by Don Nathan
Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you book an appointment through the Shears by Don Nathan app, we collect your name, phone number, salon name, salon address, and any notes you provide related to your shear sharpening service.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to schedule and confirm your shear sharpening appointment, send appointment reminders, and communicate with you about your service. We do not use your information for marketing purposes.

3. Text Messaging

By providing your phone number and booking an appointment, you consent to receive text messages from Don Nathan regarding your appointment confirmation, reminders, and service updates. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is only used to provide the shear sharpening service you have requested.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Don Nathan directly through the Shears by Don Nathan app.
